Explore Asheville

Make the most of your free time — Asheville is packed with charm, flavor, and mountain vibes.

🍺 Breweries 🏰 Biltmore 🚶 Trails 🎨 Arts 🍽 Food 🚗 Nearby

🍺 Breweries & Craft Drinks

  • Sierra Nevada Brewery — must‑visit campus, fire pits, great food
  • Wicked Weed Brewing — inventive brews, fun downtown vibe
  • Highland Brewing Co. — Asheville’s original craft brewery
  • Biltmore Park Town Square — casual taps & wine bars steps from the hotel

Not a beer fan? Try Urban Orchard Cider Co. or Chemist Gin Distillery.

🚶 Trails & Outdoors

  • Blue Ridge Parkway overlooks — short drives, big views
  • North Carolina Arboretum — peaceful trails
  • Bent Creek Experimental Forest — hiking & biking
  • Downtown Riverwalk & Greenway — easy strolls

January in the mountains = crisp air. Dress warm and layer.

🏰 Biltmore Estate & Village

  • Grounds & gardens
  • Winery tastings
  • Historic home tour
  • Village shops & dining

Tickets required — consider booking early if you'd like to visit.

🎨 Arts, Shops & Local Flavor

  • River Arts District — artists’ studios & galleries
  • Biltmore Park Town Square — shops & eateries by the hotel
  • Downtown Asheville — local shops, bookstores, cafes, buskers

🚗 Easy Adventures Nearby

  • Brevard — waterfalls & small‑town charm
  • Black Mountain — artsy shops + coffee
  • Hendersonville — wineries & mountain vibes
